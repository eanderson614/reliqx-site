Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

ReliqX ("we," "us") helps collectors value, authenticate, and track sports cards and memorabilia. This policy explains what information the ReliqX app and website collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect only what we need to run the app for you, and we do not sell your personal information.

Information we collect

  • Account & contact info. Your email address (and password) when you create an account or join the waitlist.
  • Photos you submit. Images you take or choose to identify and value an item. Photos are uploaded to our servers and processed to recognize the item and find comparable sales.
  • Collection data you enter. The items in your collection and details you add — player, team, year, grade, authenticator, cert number, purchase price and date, storage location, and notes.
  • Usage & diagnostic data. Basic technical information needed to operate and troubleshoot the app, such as app version, device type, and error logs.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, browsing history, or financial account credentials. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How we use your information

  • Identify and value the items you photograph or enter, using real sold-comparable data.
  • Store and display your collection, portfolio value, alerts, and reports.
  • Provide tax estimates and insurance quotes based on the values and details you've recorded.
  • Operate, secure, maintain, and improve the app.
  • Send you account-related or launch communications you've requested.

How your information is shared

We share information only as needed to provide the service:

  • AI image processing. Photos you submit for identification may be processed by our AI provider (Anthropic) solely to recognize the item. They are not used to train third-party models for advertising.
  • Valuation & market data sources. We query public marketplaces and auction sources (such as eBay, Goldin, PWCC, and Heritage) to find comparable sales. We send search terms derived from your item, not your personal identity.
  • Hosting & storage providers. We use reputable cloud infrastructure to host the app and store your data on your behalf.
  • eBay links. When you choose "List on eBay," the app opens eBay in your browser. We may use an affiliate link, which means eBay can pay us a commission if you make a purchase. This is an outbound link; we do not share your collection data with eBay.
  • Legal. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or ReliqX.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep your account and collection data for as long as your account is active. You can delete items or your entire account at any time; when you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with law.

Your choices and rights

  • Access & export. You can view your collection in the app and export it to CSV at any time.
  • Correction & deletion. You can edit or delete any item, and you can request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Communications. You can opt out of non-essential emails.

Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK or California), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any right, contact us using the details below.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

ReliqX is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

International users

ReliqX is operated from the United States. If you use the app from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be processed in the U.S. and other countries where we or our providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the date above. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by email where appropriate.

Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email us at support@reliqx.app.
